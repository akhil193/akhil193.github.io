  • m1 and m2

  • r
    With one regular ruler, we can measure the distance between two bodies.
  • G
    We generally take the value of G granted and say it is a CONSTANT as we just know the value. But the real questions is how did someone actually came up with such a value of a constant and how was it measured in the first place.
